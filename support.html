Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. You'll receive SMS updates at each stage of delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, and Cash on Delivery. All mobile money payments are secure and instant.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Kigali takes 2-4 hours. Outside Kigali, delivery takes 1-2 business days.

Can I return or exchange products?

Yes! You can return or exchange products within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

How do I earn loyalty points?

Earn 1 point for every 100 RWF spent. Refer friends to earn 500 bonus points. Redeem points for discounts and rewards!
