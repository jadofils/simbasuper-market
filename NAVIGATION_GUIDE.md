## 🎯 How to Access Each Feature

### 1. Support Center (FAQ)
**Desktop:**
- Click **"FAQ"** button (left side, next to logo)
- OR click **"Help"** link (right side)

**Mobile:**
- Tap **"FAQ"** in bottom navigation bar

**What you'll find:**
- Frequently Asked Questions
- Contact information (Phone, Email, WhatsApp)
- Live chat widget
- Help articles

---

### 2. Rewards & Loyalty
**Desktop:**
- Click **🎁 Gift Icon** (right side of navbar)

**Mobile:**
- Tap **"Rewards"** in bottom navigation bar

**What you'll find:**
- Points balance
- Tier status (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum)
- Redeemable rewards
- Referral program
- Activity history

---

### 3. Live Chat Support
**All Pages:**
- Look for **floating chat button** (bottom-right corner)
- Click to open chat window
- Available on ALL pages (index, cart, product, support, rewards)

**Features:**
- Instant AI responses
- Quick reply buttons
- Typing indicator
- Always accessible

---

### 4. Shopping Cart
**Desktop:**
- Click **🛒 Cart** button (right side)

**Mobile:**
- Tap **"Cart"** in bottom navigation bar

**What you'll find:**
- Cart items
- Order summary
- Checkout button
- Quantity controls

---

### 5. Product Search
**Desktop:**
- Use search bar in center of navbar
- Click **📷 camera icon** for image search
- Click **🎤 microphone icon** for voice search

**Mobile:**
- Search bar appears at top
- Image/voice search available
